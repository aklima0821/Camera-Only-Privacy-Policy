<h2>Data Collection</h2>
<p>
Camera Only does not collect personal data automatically.
</p>

<h2>Photos & Videos</h2>
<p>
Photos and videos captured using Camera Only remain on your device by default.
</p>

<p>
If you choose to upload or share a capture through Camera Only, the uploaded content may be processed
solely for the purpose of providing the requested service (such as storage, display, optimization,
or delivery).
</p>

<h2>Content Processing</h2>
<p>
Uploaded captures may be processed after upload. This processing occurs only after explicit user action
and is limited to functionality required to support the upload or sharing feature.
</p>

<p>
Camera Only does not access or process your photos or videos without your consent.
</p>

<h2>AI & Automated Processing</h2>
<p>
Camera Only does not use artificial intelligence for image analysis or profiling.
</p>

<h2>Third-Party Services</h2>
<p>
Camera Only does not use third-party advertising or tracking services.
Any third-party services used are limited to supporting user-initiated uploads.
</p>

<h2>Data Retention</h2>
<p>
Uploaded content is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.
</p>
